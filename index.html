Lancaster City

The Red Rose City

Lancaster City, the county seat of Lancaster County, PA, was the oldest inland cities in the United States. It was the national capital for one day when the Continental Congress was fleeing the invading Britons. Today, it is an impressively diverse small city that boasts a bustling night life, a growing bar and restaurant scene, and a thriving artistic community.
